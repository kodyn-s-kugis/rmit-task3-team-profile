Results Reviewed

Discussing the importance of understanding the test results for our group.

  • Next

Allowing everyone in the group to review and collate the results of each individuals test is imperative to ensure from the team's inception that we work effectively.

After everyone has established an understanding, the team can proceed to delegate tasks; this will allow the group to take advantage of people's strengths and weaknesses (e.g. assigning creative tasks such as designing or story work to someone that has a high aptitude in that characteristic).

Evaluation of people's attributes on how they approach learning, working and receiving feedback ties back into many of the tests that have been done. Taking these factors into consideration and referencing the test results allows us to tailor and prepare our interactions with others; this should prevent conflicts between team members. However, if disputes do arise, we can extrapolate on the individual's test results and approach them in a manner best suited to them.

Overall a comprehensive understanding of each other guarantees a high probability of cohesion, efficiency and organisation, all while avoiding conflicts, uncertainties and distrust.
